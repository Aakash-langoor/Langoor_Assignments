About Us

Langoor is an atypical web services and products company based in Sydney, Australia and Bangalore, India. We are made up of a dedicated team of project managers, social media geeks, website and SEO copywriters, programmers, and web and graphic designers.

Established in August 2009, our company came together with the broad objective of helping clients reach their full potential in the digital environment. We do this by building, empowering and managing your online setup, and by giving you the means with which to fully engage with your audience. We are here to help you go beyond customer engagement and create lasting relationships.

We believe in results and simplicity. We innovate. We work hard to keep our clients happy. Our humble beginnings make us small enough to genuinely care and big enough to meet any challenge no matter the size or scope.

You can see what we do in more detail in the services section, and what some of our long term internal projects and goals are in labs. If this is not your first time here and you remember our old website we have still preserved it for you to see it here.

Our portfolio will give you some background on our past work and our blog was designed to be informative, entertaining and a useful resource. Some of our work has featured in top websites, newspapers and publications across the world.

If you’d like to know if we have any positions available, check out careers, and if you’re interested in getting in touch with us, our contact page will show you how.
