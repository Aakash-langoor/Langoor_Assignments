Digital Marketing & Strategy

Our digital marketing and strategic services cover a range of specialities to make sure that your website is at its most competitive in your sector.

Website & Social Media Integration - Your visitors' experience and interaction with your website is crucial. We can help you build innovative and results driven campaigns to engage with your target audience through various forms of media. We can also make sure your website works in concert with your social media campaigns, such as Twitter and Facebook, so that your visitors immediately perceive that they are all part of the big picture.

Online Market Positioning - Working in conjunction with Search Engine Optimisation (detailed under Web Design & Development), online market positioning involves making your website competitive in your sector. The means through which this is done is up to you and we will provide you with several options geared towards building better professional online relationships, but they all fall under a larger umbrella of public relations and digital marketing.

Analytics & Tangible Goal Management - Stay in the loop! Track the traffic you receive, the time your visitors spend on your website, the process they go through to get to your website and more. We can advise you on how to infer these results so that you can capitalise on opportunities to improve your online presence.

Advertising Management - We have Google Advertising Professionals on our team, so ask us about how you might like to leverage Google AdWords for your website. Whether it is an ongoing campaign or just a placeholder until you see results from Search Engine Optimisation, or whether traffic from Google Search (text ads) or the Google Display Network (multimedia ads) or both is the best strategy, we can cater any option to your needs. We can also support and advise your ad buying and planning on other online networks.

Online Video Production - This is a unique part of what we do where clients need videos that work quickly and effectively online. We have access to video producers and editors who can create new videos or just help make existing ones effective for online viewing.
